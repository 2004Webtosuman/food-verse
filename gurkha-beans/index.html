Our Heritage

A Cup Rooted in the Himalayas

Gurkha Beans was born in the narrow lanes of Thamel, Kathmandu — where the aroma of freshly roasted beans mingles with the mountain breeze. Inspired by the legendary resilience of the Gurkha warriors, we crafted a coffee experience that is bold, warm, and deeply rooted.

Every brew tells a story of the hills — of farmers in Palpa and Gulmi who hand-pick cherries at dawn, of traditions passed through generations, and of a community that lives and breathes coffee with quiet pride.

We source our beans exclusively from small Nepali farms, supporting over 50 farming families across the mid-hills. Our roasters honor terroir, and our baristas honor craft.
